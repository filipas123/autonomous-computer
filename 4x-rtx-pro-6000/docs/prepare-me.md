# Preparation — Mechanical & Housing

The 5U chassis kit (case, internal cables, and power board) is supplied as a complete unit, so no separate CNC / sheet-metal preparation is required for this build.

| No  | Step              | Notes                                  |
|:---:|:------------------|:---------------------------------------|
|  1  | Unbox 5U chassis  | Verify case, cable harness, power board |
|  2  | Inspect fittings  | Standoffs, screws, fan brackets         |
|  3  | Stage chassis     | Place on anti-static workbench          |
